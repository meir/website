OLKB Planck + GMK Bentō

This is my third mechanical keyboard but my daily driver since i got it in 2022. It's a 40% keyboard with a 2u spacebar. Later on i ordered a custom keycap to replace the spacebar for a resin pokemon keycap ordered from Hirosart I'm absolutely in love with the spacebar i got from them, it involves a tiny shiny ditto alongside a tiny ghastly in it.

In total, this keyboard is comprised of the following components:

  • GMK Bentō - Base kit [link]
  • GMK Bentō - Salmon Novelty kit [link]
  • GMK Bentō - 40s kit [link]
  • GMK Bentō x RAMA Waves Artisan Keycap [link]
  • Gazzew Boba U4TX × 47 [link]
  • OLKB Planck (High Profile Yellow) [link]
  • Custom 2u Pokemon Keycap [link]
