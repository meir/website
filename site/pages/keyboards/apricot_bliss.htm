Update 25-07-2023

I received the keyboard a couple of weeks ago, im absolutely in love with it. Though i have changed the specs a little bit, instead of using bobagum switches and SA Bliss, i've decided to go with Owlabs Neon switches and JTK Night Sakura. In my opinion this makes a much better match and the neon switches give a very low, very satisfying thocky sound.

In total, this keyboard is comprised of the following components:
